Activities & Experiences

Live Music & DJ Sessions

Open mics, vinyl nights, and DJ sets in an intimate lounge setting. Members bring records, collaborate, and vibe.

Painting sessions, live sketching, and rotating exhibitions of local artists. Bring your canvas or just your ideas.

Yoga & Mindfulness

Weekly yoga, meditation circles, and breathwork. A calm corner for mental clarity and body balance.

Game Nights & Tapas

Chess, board games, trivia, and shared tapas evenings. Real connection, no screens required.
