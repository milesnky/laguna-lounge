Activities & Experiences

Vinyl Bar DJ Sessions

Our signature weekly event. Members bring records or spin from our collection in the cozy vinyl bar. Deep house, jazz, soul, techno — no phones, no distractions. Just pure sound and shared vibes.

Members-only · Every Friday · 8 PM – Late

Art Jams & Rotating Exhibitions

Monthly painting sessions, live sketching, and exhibitions of local artists. From abstract to street art — our walls evolve with the community. Bring your tools or just your eyes.

Last Friday of the month · All levels welcome

Science & Harm Reduction Talks

Guest experts — doctors, lawyers, cultivators — dive into cannabis science, legal rights, sustainable growing, and mental health. Open Q&A, no judgment, just knowledge.

Monthly · Expert-led · Open discussion

Tapas, Games & Third Place

Weekly game nights (chess, trivia), shared tapas, yoga circles, and meditation. This is your third place — calm, private, community-driven.

Every Thursday · Bring a dish or just yourself
