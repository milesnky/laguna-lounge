Activities & Experiences

Vinyl Bar DJ Sessions

Every week, members gather in our intimate vinyl bar for curated DJ sets and open turntables. Bring your own records or spin from our growing collection. From deep house to jazz, soul to techno — it’s about shared sound and connection. No phones. No distractions. Just the music and the moment.

Members-only · Weekly · 8 PM – Late

Art Jams & Rotating Exhibitions

Monthly art nights where members paint, sketch, or sculpt together. No experience needed — just curiosity. Our walls rotate with works from local artists: photography, abstracts, street art. A space to create, inspire, and be inspired.

Open to all levels · Last Friday of the month

Science, Law & Wellness Talks

Guest speakers — doctors, lawyers, activists, cultivators — share knowledge on cannabis science, legal rights, harm reduction, and mental health. Workshops on sustainable growing, extraction, and responsible use. Education is at the core of our responsibility.

Monthly · Expert-led · Open discussion

Tapas, Games & Third Place Vibes

Weekly game nights: chess, backgammon, cards, trivia. Shared tapas evenings with homemade dishes. Yoga circles, meditation, art therapy. This is your third place — not home, not work, but a calm, private sanctuary where you belong.

Every Thursday · Bring a dish or just yourself
