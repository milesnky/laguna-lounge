Our Purpose

Laguna Lounge CSC is a private, independent, non-profit cultural association dedicated to responsible lifestyles, creativity, community growth, and overall well-being. We exist in a legal grey area — tolerated under certain conditions, though not explicitly legalized by national law.

The “Third Place”

Sociologically, many members describe our CSC as a “third place” — not home, not work, but a safe and friendly environment where they can relax, be themselves, and connect with others. It’s calm, private, and community-driven, compared to nightlife venues or bars. Staff are trained to maintain a peaceful, respectful vibe. Members take part in maintaining the space, decorating, or organizing activities.

Purpose Beyond Cannabis

Ultimately, we position ourselves as a social, cultural, and educational space where cannabis is one part of a broader, responsible community experience. We help reduce stigma by showing cannabis users can be healthy, creative, and responsible. We build micro-communities of artists, professionals, and locals. We promote alternative culture, sustainability, and mental well-being.
