About Laguna Lounge CSC

We are a private, non-profit cultural association dedicated to responsible lifestyles, creativity, and community growth.

Operating in Spain’s legally tolerated framework, we are fully compliant with regional guidance for asociaciones culturales.

Our space is discreet, mature, and members-only — more like a private cultural lounge than a public venue.

We foster genuine human connection through shared experiences: art, music, wellness, and education.

Purpose beyond any single focus: We reduce stigma, nurture micro-communities, and champion alternative culture and mental well-being.
